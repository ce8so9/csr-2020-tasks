Share A Secret

In recent years, a vast number of online secret sharing sites has emerged.

While it is obviously a great idea to send passwords and secrets via a free-to-use website, we wanted to create an even more secure app.

We use a pre-state-of-the-art post quantum secure streuwert function in combination with a real time tangle-based KV store engine (much like the blockchain).

We even provide our source code.
